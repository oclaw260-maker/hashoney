The Hard Money Checklist

Does it hold the properties of sound money?

01 / 04
Is the supply limited by physics, not policy?
Yes. Jarrah flowers once a year, for a few weeks. No beekeeper can inflate the harvest by pressing more buttons. Bees do what bees do. The supply is fixed by biology.
02 / 04
Can it be counterfeited or diluted?
No. Pure Jarrah honey grades are lab-verifiable via pollen analysis and mass spectrometry. You cannot print AUD to buy more jarrah forest.
03 / 04
Does the producer control the supply?
No. We sell what we harvest. When the season ends, the product is gone. We don't reorder from a wholesaler. Next season's batch is genuinely uncertain — dependent on the forest, not the forecast.
04 / 04
Does it store value across time?
Yes. Raw Jarrah honey has a shelf life measured in years, not months. Sats in a hardware wallet last forever. So does real honey.
